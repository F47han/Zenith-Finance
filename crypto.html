Zenith Protocol FAQs

Review standard inquiries relating to smart contracts, wallets, and gas settings.

How does autocompounding operate?

Our automation engines gather staking yields hourly, swap them for vault principal tokens, and restake. This maximizes compound interest return yields.

Is there a lockup fee or withdrawal penalty?

We do not enforce withdrawal penalties. A flat 0.1% deposit routing fee applies to offset gas routing charges during automated contract execution.

Which wallets are supported by Zenith?

We support standard Web3 wallet configurations including MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, and Trust Wallet via EVM chain routing protocols.
